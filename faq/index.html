bot4x, answered plainly

What is bot4x?

bot4x is an AI marketing-ops operator that runs a founder's X account in their own voice, with receipt-backed drafts, auditable publish/hold judgment, and brand-safe posting mandates. The slogan says it in four words: built to post right.

Can bot4x run my X account in my own voice?

Yes. Voice is the starting point, not an afterthought. bot4x has a 20-persona voice library: three voices are free, you can pick any two voices and fuse them into one, and a fully custom voice is available. The goal is posts that sound like you on your best day — we want the best of you.

Does bot4x publish automatically?

bot4x is not a blind autoposter. Every draft is judged against your mandate before anything carries your name, and every publish or hold decision is logged. You can add an approval step so nothing goes live without your sign-off.

What is a mandate?

A mandate is the signed brief your account runs under: who you are talking to, what you talk about, the voice you use, the claims you are willing to make, and the lines you never cross. Every judgment the operator makes is checked against it.

What counts as a receipt?

A receipt is a verifiable fact behind a post: a real event, a real number from your work, a real interaction someone could check. Drafts are built from receipts, so every claim in a published post traces back to something true.

How does bot4x avoid brand risk?

Three layers. The mandate defines what your account will and will not say. Receipts keep every claim anchored to something verifiable. And auditable publish/hold judgment means each post is a logged decision, not a reflex — you can open the audit log and see why anything went out. More on the brand-safe AI posting page.

Who is bot4x for?

Boutique B2B consultants, fractional executives, and solo B2B founders — people whose name is the product, who want to publish consistently on X without outsourcing their judgment or flattening their voice.

How is bot4x different from a ghostwriter?

A ghostwriter lends you their voice and their instincts. bot4x runs yours: it writes from your receipts, in a voice built from your register, under a mandate you set — and it leaves an audit trail a ghostwriter never could. See how the operator works.

How is bot4x different from a scheduler?

A scheduler moves finished posts into time slots. bot4x sits upstream and does the part that actually protects you: deciding what to say, when to say it, and what to leave out — then writing it in your voice with a receipt behind it.

What are the three public boards?

bot4x demonstrates itself in public on three boards: Build-in-Public (Don't just build your dream / Tell us more while you build it), Before / After / Rule demos (we show our work), and The Letter — a long, plain-language letter to our future client. The Build-in-Public board has its own page: build in public automation.

Who builds bot4x?

A solo founder. The product runs its own X account, @bot4x_, under the same mandate discipline it offers clients — the account is the live demo.
