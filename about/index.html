Who it is for

Boutique B2B consultants, fractional executives, and solo B2B founders — people whose name is the product. If your reputation is what clients are actually buying, your X account cannot be an experiment in generic AI output.

What it does

bot4x turns real client and founder signals into X posts. Not invented content: real events from your work — a note you wrote, a decision you made, a question a prospect asked — become drafts in your voice, each one anchored to the thing that actually happened.

What makes it safe

  • Every post has a receipt — a verifiable fact behind the claim.
  • Every publish/hold decision is logged, so the judgment is auditable after the fact.
  • Every account runs under a mandate — a signed brief that defines what your account says, in what voice, to whom, and where the lines are.

Voice

A 20-persona voice library. Three voices are free. Pick any two and fuse them into one voice that fits you. A fully custom voice is available for accounts that need an exact register. The promise behind all of it: we want the best of you.

How it shows its work in public

  • Build-in-Public — Don't just build your dream / Tell us more while you build it. Building moments, told right, one point and one true receipt per post. More on the build in public automation page.
  • Before / After / Rule — we show our work. A real draft, the stronger rewrite, and the one rule you can reuse.
  • The Letter — to our future client. A long, plain-language letter to someone we chose: why them, what we would do, what to expect — humbly.

Who builds it
