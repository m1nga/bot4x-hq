Brand-safe AI posting starts with a mandate

bot4x is an AI marketing-ops operator that runs a founder's X account in their own voice, with receipt-backed drafts, auditable publish/hold judgment, and brand-safe posting mandates.

Most tools treat brand safety as a checklist run at the end. bot4x treats brand-safe AI posting as the design itself: what to say, when to say it, and what to leave out — decided by judgment you agreed to in advance, and written down every time.

The mandate: what your account stands for

Every bot4x account runs under a mandate — a signed, machine-readable brief. It names your audience, your topics, your voice, the claims you are willing to make, and the lines your account never crosses. It is not a vibe document; the operator checks every judgment against it. When your account speaks, it speaks inside the mandate.

Receipts: every claim traces to something real

Each draft is built on a receipt — a real event, a real number from your work, a real interaction someone could verify. Receipts are what separate a confident-sounding account from an accountable one: any sentence that goes out under your name can show where it came from.

Publish/hold judgment, with approval when you want it

Before anything carries your name, the draft gets a decision: publish or hold. Hold is an editorial call — the way a magazine holds a strong story for the issue where it lands best. The decision is made against your mandate, and you can add an approval step so nothing goes live without your explicit sign-off. Autopilot is optional; judgment is not.

The audit log: every decision on the record

Every publish/hold decision is logged with its reasoning and its receipt. When someone asks "why did the account say that?", there is an answer with a timestamp. That audit log is the difference between trusting a tool and being able to check one — auditable judgment is the product, not a feature flag.

Who this protects

Boutique B2B consultants, fractional executives, and solo B2B founders — people for whom one careless post costs more than a quarter of good ones earn. If your name is the brand, the account that carries it should run under a mandate.
